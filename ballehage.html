Explore the picturesque Ballehage Beach

A natural gem place to let your ideas flow, long yoga/ exercise routines, meditation and more

There are different bodies of water like there are diverse ways for yoga: a lake is still, a river is flowing. Like the energy in the body, the sea is constantly changing and it's all connected through a channel that ends at the sea. Visit this public long beach at any time of the year, it will be always a different panorama. Danish painters have illustrated the surrounding beaches for a reason.

This place could be perfect for you

If you feel connected with the water element, enjoy feeling the breeze, sand under your feet, hearing waves sounds and water birds chirping, and smelling the salt ocean water this is the place for you to discover.

What you will find there

There is parking for bikes, changing rooms and restrooms in the area. The beach is open for public all year long but even in summer you can find a spot to relax. On your own or with a group of friends, there is always a place for everyone.
